# Competitive Analysis Key Findings

## Features Competitors Have That AXIOM CRM Currently Lacks

### TMS/Operations Features (from Tai TMS, DAT, McLeod, EZ Loader, Rose Rocket, Alchemy)
- Load management (create, track, dispatch loads)
- Carrier vetting & performance scoring
- Digital freight matching / capacity matching
- Predictive ETAs
- Automated invoicing & carrier bill audit
- LTL portal (rating, dispatching, tracking)
- Dock scheduling
- Route optimization
- Load board integration (DAT, Truckstop, 123Loadboard)
- EDI support (204, 210, 214)
- Freight rate management (multi-modal)
- Driver management & settlements
- Fleet management
- Fuel management
- Claims management
- Digital customer onboarding with e-signatures
- White-labeling capabilities
- Customer portal (self-service quotes, tracking, tender)
- Freight audit automation

### AI/Intelligence Features (from Outreach, Apollo, Salesforce, Rose Rocket)
- AI-powered order entry from emails/PDFs (Rose Rocket TED)
- Conversation intelligence / live call monitoring (Outreach)
- AI forecast projections & scenario planning (Outreach)
- Mutual action plans (Outreach)
- Anonymous website visitor identification (Apollo)
- Buyer intent data signals (Apollo)
- 275M+ B2B contact database (Apollo)
- AI agents for rate quotes, booking, scheduling (Salesforce Agentforce)
- No-code workflow builder for custom automation (Rose Rocket)

### Communication Features (from Apollo, Outreach)
- Email warmup built-in (Apollo)
- A/Z testing (multi-variant) (Apollo)
- Unified inbox across channels (Apollo)
- Conversation intelligence with call recording & AI insights (Outreach)

## What AXIOM CRM Already Does Better Than Most Competitors

1. **AI Voice Agent** - No competitor has a built-in AI calling agent
2. **Document Intelligence (DocScan)** - Most TMS platforms have basic doc parsing, AXIOM has AI-powered extraction
3. **Win Probability Engine** - Only Outreach has deal health scores; AXIOM has full AI scoring
4. **Revenue Autopilot** - Unique daily AI briefings with prioritized actions
5. **AI Ghostwriter** - Only Apollo has AI writing; AXIOM has context-aware freight-specific drafting
6. **Paradigm Engine (BNB Prospecting)** - No competitor has autonomous AI prospecting with psychographic profiling
7. **Ghost Mode (Autonomous Sequences)** - More advanced than Apollo/Outreach sequences with AI personalization
8. **Compliance Fortress** - Most comprehensive CAN-SPAM/GDPR/CCPA compliance engine
9. **Deliverability Infrastructure** - Provider-specific optimization (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo) exceeds Apollo's warmup
10. **Multi-Tenant SaaS Architecture** - Built for resale from day one with role hierarchy
11. **DOT/FMCSA Broker Scanner** - Unique feature for finding new/renewing brokers
12. **Quantum Lead Score** - 12-dimension scoring model exceeds any competitor

## Critical Gaps to Address for Market Competitiveness

### Must-Have (competitors all have these)
1. Load management / load tracking
2. Carrier management with DOT/FMCSA vetting
3. Load board integration (DAT, Truckstop)
4. Basic invoicing / billing
5. Customer portal (self-service)

### Nice-to-Have (differentiators some competitors have)
1. White-labeling for sub-brokers
2. EDI support
3. Route optimization
4. Freight rate intelligence / rate comparison
5. LTL portal
6. E-signature support for documents
7. Anonymous website visitor tracking
