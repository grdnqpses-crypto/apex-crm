## Core CRM Module

The foundation of REALM CRM is a modern, full-featured customer relationship management system built specifically for freight brokers.

### Contacts
The contact management system stores every shipper, carrier, driver, and business relationship in one place. Each contact record includes standard fields (name, email, phone, company) plus freight-specific fields like MC number, DOT number, equipment types, and lane preferences. Contacts are automatically enriched with data from the B2B Database and scored by the Win Probability Engine.

### Companies
Company records aggregate all contacts, deals, loads, and communication history for each business relationship. The system tracks company-level metrics like total revenue, average margin, payment history, and relationship health score. Companies can be tagged, segmented, and targeted for campaigns.

### Deals
The deal pipeline tracks every opportunity from initial contact through closed-won. Deals flow through customizable pipeline stages with drag-and-drop Kanban boards. Each deal is automatically scored by the Win Probability Engine, which predicts the likelihood of closing based on historical patterns, engagement signals, and market conditions.

### Tasks
The task management system ensures nothing falls through the cracks. Tasks can be created manually or generated automatically by the AI — for example, when the Revenue Autopilot identifies a follow-up opportunity or when a carrier document is about to expire.

### Pipelines
Multiple customizable pipelines support different business processes — one for shipper acquisition, one for carrier onboarding, one for load execution. Each pipeline has its own stages, automation rules, and reporting.

## Operations Center

The Operations Center is where daily freight brokerage work happens. This is the module that makes REALM CRM a true TMS (Transportation Management System), not just a CRM.

### Load Management
The load management system handles the complete lifecycle of every shipment:

- **Create loads** with origin, destination, commodity, weight, equipment type, and rate
- **Dispatch loads** to vetted carriers with one click
- **Track loads** in real-time with status updates (booked, dispatched, in transit, delivered)
- **View load history** with complete audit trail of every status change
- **Automated load board posting** — loads are automatically posted to DAT, Truckstop, and 123Loadboard

When a load is created, the system automatically suggests the best carrier based on lane history, safety rating, insurance status, and availability. The AI Voice Agent can even call carriers to negotiate rates and confirm availability.

### Carrier Vetting
The carrier vetting system integrates with FMCSA data to provide real-time safety and compliance verification:

- **DOT/MC number lookup** with instant authority verification
- **Safety rating** pulled directly from FMCSA databases
- **Insurance verification** with automatic expiration alerts
- **Operating authority status** — active, inactive, or revoked
- **Inspection history** and out-of-service rates
- **Carrier scoring** based on safety, reliability, and payment history

Every carrier in the system is continuously monitored. If a carrier's insurance lapses or their safety rating changes, Smart Notifications immediately alert the broker and the carrier is automatically flagged in all pending loads.

### Invoicing
The invoicing system automates the billing process from load completion to payment collection:

- **Auto-generate invoices** from completed loads with all required details
- **Track payment status** — draft, sent, paid, overdue
- **Automated payment reminders** sent through Smart Notifications
- **Revenue reporting** with margin analysis per load, lane, and customer
- **Integration-ready** for QuickBooks and other accounting systems

### Customer Portal
The Customer Portal gives shippers self-service access to their freight operations:

- **Submit quote requests** directly through the portal
- **Track active loads** in real-time
- **View and pay invoices** online
- **Access historical shipment data** and reports
- **Branded experience** — the portal uses the broker's white-label branding

### Digital Onboarding
The digital onboarding system streamlines new customer and carrier setup:

- **Customizable onboarding flows** with step-by-step wizards
- **Digital document collection** — W-9s, insurance certificates, carrier agreements
- **E-signature support** for contracts and agreements
- **Automated follow-up** if onboarding steps are incomplete
- **Welcome messages** and orientation materials

### AI Order Entry
The AI Order Entry system converts inbound emails into load orders automatically:

- **Email parsing** — the AI reads incoming emails from shippers and extracts load details
- **Automatic load creation** — origin, destination, commodity, weight, and dates are populated
- **Human review** — the broker reviews and approves the AI-generated load before dispatch
- **Learning system** — the AI improves its parsing accuracy over time based on corrections

This feature alone can save a broker 2-3 hours per day on data entry, which at an average of 15,700 loads per year [2] translates to significant time savings.

## How Features Work Together Autonomously

This is the most important section of this guide. REALM CRM is not a collection of separate tools — it is an **autonomous operating system** where every feature feeds and strengthens every other feature. Here are the key autonomous workflows:

### Workflow 1: Lead to Load (Fully Autonomous)
1. **Visitor Tracking** identifies a shipping company visiting the website
2. **B2B Database** enriches the visitor with contact information
3. A **Prospect** is automatically created in the Paradigm Engine
4. **Ghost Sequences** begin automated outreach
5. **AI Voice Agent** calls the prospect with a personalized script
6. When the prospect responds, a **Deal** is created in the CRM
7. **Win Probability** scores the deal and recommends next steps
8. **AI Ghostwriter** drafts a proposal
9. When the deal closes, a **Load** is created in Operations
10. The load is posted to **Load Boards** automatically
11. **Carrier Vetting** identifies qualified carriers
12. The load is dispatched and tracked through delivery
13. An **Invoice** is generated and sent through the **Customer Portal**
14. **Smart Notifications** follow up on payment

**The broker's role:** Review and approve at key decision points. The AI handles everything else.

### Workflow 2: Carrier Onboarding (Fully Autonomous)
1. A carrier is found through the **B2B Database** or **Load Board** response
2. **FMCSA Scanner** verifies their authority, insurance, and safety rating
3. **Digital Onboarding** sends a customized onboarding flow with e-signatures
4. **DocScan** processes uploaded documents (insurance, W-9, carrier agreement)
5. **Carrier Packets** assembles a complete, presentation-ready packet
6. The carrier is added to the **Carrier Vetting** system for continuous monitoring
7. **Smart Notifications** alert if any documents expire or safety ratings change

### Workflow 3: Daily Revenue Optimization (Fully Autonomous)
1. **Revenue Autopilot** generates a morning briefing with prioritized actions
2. **Win Probability** identifies deals at risk of stalling
3. **Smart Notifications** surface overdue invoices and expiring documents
4. **AI Voice Agent** campaigns are scheduled for the day's outreach
5. **Email Campaigns** send follow-up sequences to warm leads
6. **Conversation Intelligence** analyzes yesterday's calls and provides coaching insights
7. **Meeting Prep** generates briefings for today's scheduled meetings

### Workflow 4: New Brokerage Setup (60 Seconds)
1. Broker creates account and selects a plan (2 months free)
2. **One-Touch Migration** imports all data from their existing platform
3. **White-Label Branding** applies their company identity
4. **Digital Onboarding** sets up team members with appropriate roles
5. **Email Warmup** begins preparing their sending domains
6. **Command Center** activates and generates first recommendations
7. The brokerage is operational within minutes

## The Autonomous Freight Marketplace

The Freight Marketplace is REALM CRM's most revolutionary feature — a **fully autonomous digital freight exchange** built directly into the platform. This is not a load board. This is an AI-powered marketplace where manufacturers and distributors post loads for free, the system matches them with vetted carriers, handles all paperwork autonomously, processes payments, and tracks delivery from pickup to drop-off.

### How It Works for Manufacturers & Distributors (FREE)

Manufacturers and distributors access the marketplace at zero cost. They simply fill out a load posting form with their shipment details — commodity, weight, equipment type, origin, destination, pickup and delivery dates, and their desired rate. The moment they click "Post Load," the REALM AI takes over.

| Step | What Happens | Who Does It | Time |
|------|-------------|-------------|------|
| 1. Post Load | Shipper enters freight details | Shipper | 2 minutes |
| 2. AI Carrier Matching | System analyzes 50+ factors to find the best carrier | AI | 30 seconds |
| 3. Bid Review | Top 3 carrier matches presented with scores and rates | AI | Instant |
| 4. Accept & Book | Best carrier selected, load booked | Broker/AI | 1 click |
| 5. Document Generation | BOL, rate confirmation, carrier packet, insurance cert — all generated | AI | Instant |
| 6. Payment Collection | Shipper pays at time of shipping via credit card, ACH, or wire | System | Automatic |
| 7. Pickup Confirmation | Carrier confirms pickup, tracking begins | Carrier | Real-time |
| 8. Live Tracking | GPS tracking from origin to destination, visible to shipper and broker | System | Continuous |
| 9. Delivery Confirmation | POD captured, delivery confirmed | Carrier | Real-time |
| 10. Carrier Payment | Carrier paid their fee after delivery confirmation | System | Automatic |

The broker keeps the margin between the shipper rate and the carrier rate — automatically calculated and deposited. No manual invoicing. No chasing payments. No paperwork.

### AI Carrier Matching Engine

The carrier matching algorithm evaluates every available carrier across multiple dimensions: route expertise (how often they run this lane), safety record (FMCSA data), equipment match, insurance coverage, on-time delivery history, and price competitiveness. Each carrier receives a **match score from 0-100**, and the top 3 matches are presented with full reasoning for why they were selected.

### Autonomous Document Generation

When a load is booked, the system automatically generates all required paperwork:

- **Bill of Lading (BOL)** — Complete with shipper, consignee, commodity, weight, and special instructions
- **Rate Confirmation** — Locked-in rates for both shipper and carrier
- **Carrier Packet** — Insurance certificates, authority verification, W-9, and operating agreement
- **Insurance Certificate** — Verified and current, pulled from the carrier's FMCSA record

All documents are stored in the system and accessible to all parties at any time.

### Payment Processing & Escrow

The payment flow is fully automated with built-in escrow protection:

1. **Shipper pays at booking** — The shipper's payment is collected and held in escrow
2. **Escrow funded** — Funds are secured before the carrier dispatches
3. **Delivery confirmed** — Once POD is captured, the escrow releases
4. **Carrier paid** — The carrier receives their rate minus the platform margin
5. **Margin deposited** — Your profit is automatically calculated and available

### Real-Time Tracking

Every load in the marketplace is tracked in real-time from pickup to delivery. Both the shipper and the broker can see:

- Current GPS location of the carrier
- Estimated time of arrival (ETA)
- Miles remaining
- Checkpoint history (pickup, in-transit, delay, delivery)
- Exception alerts (late pickup, route deviation, weather delays)
