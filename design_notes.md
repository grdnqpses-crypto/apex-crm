# Apex CRM Visual Redesign Notes

## Design Direction
- Warm, premium, inviting — like Crextio (image 3) and the peach SaaS dashboard (image 6)
- Soft warm background (#FBF8F4 cream/warm white), not stark white
- Warm accent colors: deep navy blue primary, warm amber/gold accents, soft coral for highlights
- Rounded corners (16px+), generous padding, soft shadows with warm tones
- Cards with subtle warm gradients, not flat gray
- Typography: Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans — clean, modern, friendly
- Status colors remain enterprise standard but rendered beautifully with soft backgrounds
- Sidebar: warm off-white with subtle gradient, not flat gray
- Welcome greeting with user name at top of dashboard
- Big beautiful stat cards with subtle gradient backgrounds
- Micro-animations on hover for warmth and interactivity
