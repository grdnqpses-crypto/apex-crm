# AXIOM CRM — Final Simplified Pricing Model

## The Golden Rule
> **Simple prices. Generous features. Premium only where it costs us real money.**

Since AI runs on Manus infrastructure (zero marginal cost to us), AI is **generous across all tiers.**
The only things we charge premium for are features with **real operational cost** (SMTP infrastructure, voice telephony, dedicated IPs) or **our highest-value proprietary engines.**
